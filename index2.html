Our Working Process

01

Discovery Call

We begin with a discovery call to understand your business needs, challenges, and goals. This initial conversation allows us to customize our AI solutions to meet your specific requirements.

02

Proposal Development

After the discovery call, we develop a detailed proposal outlining the AI functionalities tailored to your business. This includes a comprehensive plan for integration, specifying how our solutions will address your needs and enhance your operations.

03

Detailed Presentation Call

We present the detailed proposal to you, discussing the specific functionalities and agreeing on the integration plan. This call ensures that every aspect of the AI solution aligns with your business needs and expectations.

04

Product Development

In this stage, we develop the AI functionalities with a philosophy of close collaboration with our clients. We design and build the product while creating a detailed user manual to ensure smooth implementation and use.

05

Product Presentation

We present the developed product to you, gather feedback, and make necessary revisions. Additional functionalities are incorporated as needed to ensure the solution meets your expectations.

06

Implementation

We implement the AI solution into your business, ensuring seamless integration with your existing systems and processes. Our team provides support throughout this stage to guarantee a smooth transition.

07

Ongoing Support and Development

We offer ongoing support and continuously enhance the product, incorporating new technologies and functionalities as they emerge. This ensures that your AI solution remains cutting-edge and continues to meet your evolving business needs.
